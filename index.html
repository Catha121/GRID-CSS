MEDICINES AND THEIR FUNCTIONS
1. Antibiotics: this are the kind of medicines that kill or inhibit the growth of microorganisms ,such as bacteria.One of the examples can include Penicillin
2.Antidepressants: The function of this one is to treat depression ,anxiety, and other mood disorders.Examples can include Fluoxetine.
3. Antacids: They neutralize stomach acid and relieve heartburn and indigestion.They help in promoting a well functioning digestive system.
4.Cardiovascular medicines: They treat conditions related to heart and blood vessels, such as high blood pressure, high cholesterol, and heart failure.Examples include Statins, Beta blockers, Nitrates.
5.Vaccines: They prevent infectious diseases by stimulating the immune system to produce antibodies. Examples can be Flue vaccine, MMR vaccine and COVID-19 vaccine.
6. Antihistamines: They block the action of histamine, a chemical released during allergic reactions.Examples may include Loratadine and Diphenhydramine
7.Antihypertensives: They lower blood pressure and treat hypertension.Examples may include Beta blockers, Calcium channel blockers and ACE inhibitors.
